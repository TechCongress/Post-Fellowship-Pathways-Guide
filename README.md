# TechCongress Post Fellowship Pathways Guide

An interactive guide for TechCongress fellows transitioning out of their fellowship, featuring:

- **Career Path Exploration** - Six career personas with typical roles and example paths
- **Skills & Transitions** - Transferable skills assessment and sector-specific guidance
- **Job Preparation Resources** - Resume/cover letter tips, interview prep, and salary negotiation advice
- **Professional Development** - Organizations, career coaches, and networking resources
- **Looking to the Future** - Alumni mentorship and ways to stay connected

## View the Guide

Visit: https://techcongress.github.io/Post-Fellowship-Pathways-Guide/
## About TechCongress

[TechCongress](https://www.techcongress.io/) places technologists in Congress to shape tech policy and bridge the gap between technology and government.
